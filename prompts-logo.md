ultra-minimalist luxury beauty logo. The letter S formed by a single continuous flowing line of straight silky hair, the top curve of the S transforms into a subtle hair strand that falls elegantly. Beneath it, the word "SIMPLY" in thin uppercase serif letters, and "STRAIGHT" in bolder serif with the letter A stylized as a subtle hair pin. Color palette: warm rose gold and deep espresso brown.
